Professional Experience



IZARA - Technical Engineer

ERP Systems are at the core of every company. More and more firms implement cloud-driven ERP solutions by providers like SAP or Microsoft. Business processes depend on a functioning IT environment and a good understanding of modern ERP Systems is crucial when working in IT. AT PwC and Capgemini, I have seen how IT consultants work and I have realized that much of their work is concerned with Project management, rather than technology. Instead of increasing the knowledge of IT systems and concrete software, many consultant seem to build project management skills in particular. However, I strongly believe that you need a very good understanding of technology to make the right business decisions and to consult firms properly. This is why I am particulary happy about landing this student job at IZARA. In this position, I am responsible for the Application Management of the Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations system of three companies. This position gives me the opportunity to develop my technical skills and increase my understanding of ERP systems. I very much enjoy working with engineers and consultants on technical issues and I am glad that IZARA has given me this work opportunity.



PricewaterhouseCoopers - IT Consultant

After I got first insights in project management and the Internet of Things technology, I could not wait two learn more about emerging technologies. PwC gave me the opportunity to participate in the audit of SAP's cloud solutions and thus I got to know the basics of Cloud Computing as well as how a big software corporation assures the trust service principles security, availability and confidentiality. During the audit, I was mainly involved in auditing the SAP HANA Enterprise Cloud but also supported other teams in auditing S/4 HANA, ByD and IBP. My internship covered not only planning but also execution and completion of the entire audit. I learned a lot about Cloud Computing, project management and cloud audits during my time at PricewaterhouseCoopers.

Business & Technology Innovation! After I graduated from University I applied for different technology related internships.
