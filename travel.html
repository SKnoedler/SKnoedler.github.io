Thailand - Laos - Vietnam - Philippines - Malaysia - Cambodia - Indonesia

I took the plane, bus, van, boat, ferry, bicycle - I climbed, swam and walked. It has just been an amazing and unforgettable journey where I met people from uncountable many countries. 01/2018 - 05/2018

Studying and Traveling in Central & East Europe

Slovenia - Austria - Italy - Hungary - Serbia - Croatia

I studied for one semester in Ljubljana, Slovenia and really enjoyed the time this small country. Why Ljubljana? The university might not be as recognized as other universities but for me other things counted more. Ljubljana has been my first choice and I was glad to get admitted at the University of Ljubljana. It is a beautiful small country with great nature and open-minded, friendly people. I learned a lot about Slovenia and other countries in central/east Europe that I visited during my time abroad. Unfortunately, many of those countries do not get much attention from their european neighbors. However, they are definitely worth visiting.
