Data Science

I think four core skills are highly important in Data Science. Therefore, I put my focus on these four skills and believe that you should always remain curious and keep learning.

1) Problem Structuring Skills

Being able to take complex problems and turn it into a clear structure is highly important. The question are: What is the real objective? How can the objective be measured? How to link algorithms and data to the business? It is crucial to understand the business needs and be able to translate a business need or problem into an analytical problem that can be solved by data.

2) Technical Skills

This refers to the core technical skills of a Data Scientists. He/she needs to feel comfortable working with math, programming, and statistics but the person also needs to be able to make use of different tools that can meet the business needs and give appropriate answer to the analytical problem.

3) Analytical Skills

Data scientists need to be able to see patterns and trends in the data and explain those patterns. Moreover, the thinking process needs to be logically sound and complete. It is important that the Data Scientist recognizes the whole problem and finds the best suitable approach to tackle it.

4) Communication Skills

Data Scientists needs to be able to communicate data to people, explain the importance of patterns, and suggest solutions. It involves explaining complex technical issues in a way that is easy to understand. Often, this requires visual, oral, & written communication skills. Moreover, Data Science requires to collaborate and the person needs to be able to provide a clear description of work & group presentations.
