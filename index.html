Data Science

I think there are four core skills in Data Science that are most important. I strive to improve these skills and believe that you should never stop learning.

1) Problem Structuring Skills

Beeing able to take complex problems and turn it into a clear structure is highly important. The question is: What is the real objective? How can the objective be measured? How to linkalgorithms and data to the business? It is important to understand the business needs and translate the need into an anlytical problem that can be solved by data.

2) Technical Skills

This refers to the core technical skills of a Data Scientsts. A Data Scientist needs to have very strong technical skills and beeing able to produced easily readable code very on a high level.

3) Analytical Skills

The thinking process needs to be logically sound and complete. It is important to recognize the whole problem and find the best suitable approach to tackle it.

4) Communication Skills

A Data Scientists needs not only to be able to sell the derived insights and open to collaborate with many different people. A clear description of work as well as the ability to speak in front of groups are very important skills
