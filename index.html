This is me

I would ike to introduce myself based on four core skills. I believe these skills are highly important when working with data and developing software

1) Problem Solving and Logical Thinking

Programmers spend a high percentage of their time debugging rather than writing code. Therefore, being able to not only pinpoint software problems but also use deductive reasoning to solve these issues is crucial.

2) Technical Skills

This refers to the core technical skills of an Engineer. He/she needs to feel comfortable working with programming and infrastrcuture but the person also needs to be able to make use of different tools that can meet the business needs and give appropriate answer to analytical problems.

3) Passion & Intrinsic Motivation

I strongly believe that you can learn everything if you have enough time and motivation. When it comes to many fields in engineering, your motivation and dedication sets you apart from other employees. It can help you learn new skills and master them.

4) Communication & Teamplaying

Engineers needs to be able to communicate to people, explain issues, and suggest solutions. It involves explaining complex technical issues in a way that is easy to understand. Often, this requires visual, oral, & written communication skills. Moreover, engineering requires to collaborate and teamwork is a curcial part of the work.
